Legendary weapon in Cyberpunk 2077

Unique weapons are only one part of the fun of playing Cyberpunk 2077. They often have a lot of interesting and powerful effects, making them the main tools for the entire gameplay of the game. In addition, there is a wide variety of unique weapons in the game, so no matter what type of character the player chooses, there is a suitable unique weapon for them. And of course, all of us, when we first launched the game and saw the wall in V's hiding place, wanted to fill it completely. This guide will help you with this task.
